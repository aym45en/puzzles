Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly known as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded on March 6, 1902
Real Madrid has an illustrious history of achievements, both domestically and internationally. 
Over the years, Real Madrid has been home to some of the greatest football players in history.
The term "Galácticos" refers to a period in the early 2000s when Real Madrid signed several high-profile stars.
Real Madrid has several intense rivalries, the most famous being with FC Barcelona.
Real Madrid's youth academy, known as La Fábrica (The Factory), has produced numerous talented players who have gone on to achieve great success.
In recent years, Real Madrid has continued to enjoy success both domestically and in Europe.
Real Madrid has been led by some of the most respected managers in football history.
